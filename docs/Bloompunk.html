Bloompunk

A hardcore FPS Roguelike with a core theme of growth. Abilities that are picked up along the run grow onto the player, and must be fed by completing "hunger quests" to keep them from withering and dying.

I am working as the lead engineer, overseeing a team of 5 other engineers and managing the overall cohesion and organization of the codebase, as well as personally handling game flow and UI implementation.

Bloompunk is made in Unity as a part of the USC Advanced Games Project, with release planned for Spring 2023.

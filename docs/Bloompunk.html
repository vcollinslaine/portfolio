Bloompunk

Lead Engineer

official website

    Bloompunk is a resource-management FPS roguelike with a core theme of growth.

    It takes place in a world of plants and steampunk, where your weapons & abilities are alive and grow on your body.

    It was made as part of the USC Advanced Games Project capstone course from September 2022 to May 2023.

    I worked as the lead engineer, leading a team of 5 others. In addition to leadership responsibilities, I was directly responsible for much of the user interface and game flow implementation.

03 : About Me

Hi! I'm V. I'm currently in my third year of studying game design at the University of Southern California. I'm primarily an engineer, although I have experience in design as well.

I'm currently working on the USC Advanced Games Project Bloompunk as the lead engineer. I also co-founded and was the sole engineer for Blue Monarch Games, which is releasing an adventure game about old gods and delivering mail, called Keep Me Posted! soon.

Although I've developed games across a wide range of genres, I'm particularly passionate about fighting games. I particularly like working in areas of systems design, tools development, and gameplay engineering.

official website

    Blue Monarch Games was the summer project of me and 5 of my friends.

    We holed up at my childhood home in New Hampshire for two months with questionable internet and set out to make a game.

    Over those 8 weeks, we made Keep Me Posted, a game about old gods, delivering mail, and drifting away from somebody.

    I was the only engineer on the team. In addition to the vast majority of the code in the game, I also coordinated my team's usage of git.

    Keep Me Posted was successfully Kickstarted and launched in October of 2022.

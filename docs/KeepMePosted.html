Blue Monarch Games

Co-Founder, Solo Engineer

official website

    Blue Monarch Games was the summer project of me and 5 of my friends.

    We holed up at my childhood home in New Hampshire for two months with questionable internet and set out to make a game.

    Over those 8 weeks, we made Keep Me Posted, a game about old gods, delivering mail, and drifting away from somebody.

    I was the only engineer on the team. In addition to the vast majority of the code in the game, I also coordinated my team's usage of git.

    Keep Me Posted was successfully Kickstarted and launched in October 2022.

A screenshot showing the day and task UI

Engineering

  • Created custom ScriptableObject-based state machine for player and enemy logic
  • Implemented quest progression systems with save slots
  • Managed team's git usage, including recovering from a server crash
  • Coded state-based enemy AI using A* and navmesh pathfinding for movement
  • Handled runtime chunk loading and unloading
  • Coordinated build pipelines and Steam uploads

Other Contributions

  • Assisted in overarching experience design and ideation conversations
  • Prototyped systems to test and iterate on design decisions
  • Collaborated on combat design decisions for player attacks and enemy behaviour
  • Coordinated Kickstarter rewards and updates
  • Handled communication with Indiecade and USC Games Expo

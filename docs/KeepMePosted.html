Keep Me Posted!

An action-adventure game where you play as a mailman for a forest village (and you're a mouse). You have to get past aggressive (but cute!) eldritch critters to deliver mail, and sometimes that mail is addressed to an ancient god. Talk with your neighbors, deliver mail, and help an inconceivably old being deal with a long-distance friendship.

Keep Me Posted! is a game by Blue Monarch Games, a studio that I co-founded. I was also the sole engineer, and participated heavily in the design process. Blue Monarch Games was an attempt by me and 5 of my classmates to try and create a game over a summer in a healthy work environment.

Keep Me Posted was made in Unity over the course of 8 weeks, and a Kickstarter campaign will be starting within the month.
